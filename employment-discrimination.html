Employment Discrimination

If you've lost your job because of unlawful discrimination, give us a call. While Colorado is an at-will state, there are many circumstances where your termination may have been illegal. When there is discrimination based on race, gender, age, religion, disability, national origin, pregnancy, free speech, or some other factor, employers should never get away with it. We will do everything possible to make sure they don't.

We're here to help you restore your dignity and get you a remedy when you've been wronged. At our core, we are trial lawyers. On the one hand, we have achieved successful, lucrative settlements for many previous clients. On the other, if your employer is unwilling to settle, we’re ready for court. We have conducted upwards of 80 jury trials, many with exceptionally high stakes. We are battle-tested fighters and there is nothing that intimidates us, because in the end, vindicating your cause is what we care about.
