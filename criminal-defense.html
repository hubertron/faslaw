Criminal Defense

"BEAT BACK CONVICTIONS...
BATTER DOWN SENTENCES."
-Eugene V. Debs

At Frank and Salahuddin LLC, our guiding principle is to beat back convictions and batter down sentences. We work smarter and harder to get to know you, your goals, and your time frame, so we can effectively and efficiently present your defense. Every attorney at Frank and Salahuddin LLC is an experienced, highly successful trial lawyer with the wins to prove it.

Because no two cases or clients are alike, we develop a unique strategy for each client. Sometimes the strategy involves sharing the results of our investigation with a district attorney to secure a good result before trial; other times it means aggressively litigating every possible issue under the sun – going to war. We leave no stone unturned, continuously working at persuading the prosecution to make a favorable offer while also painstakingly preparing your case to win at trial.

We defend criminal cases because we care deeply about the lives of the people the state is seeking to punish. We believe that people are more than the worst accusation they've faced. And not only are we ready to fight, we love the fight.
